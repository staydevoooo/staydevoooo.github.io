What is Luna?

Luna is a free-to-use proxy with the goal of providing the user with the ability to access any website, for example at work or school when the website is blocked by your ISP. Luna has it's own built in vpn to encrypt your connection, blocking attackers from accessing your device. *DISCLAIMER* Devoooo is not responsible for any trouble you may get in by accessing blocked sites.
